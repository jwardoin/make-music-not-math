Whether you like it or not, every time you make money on a gig, teaching a lesson, or selling an album, that is considered income by the IRS and thus is subject to income tax laws. In most cases, this income will be classified as self-employed or contract income; this means that on top of paying income tax, you are also subject to paying the full amount for FICA tax (Social Security and Medicare).

Using this page, you can quickly get an idea of how much tax you will owe
